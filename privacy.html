Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to DataURI.ai ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you use our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure.

Key Point: Our image-to-Data URL converter processes all files entirely in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, and we do not store any of your converted files or Data URLs.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

Our core converter tool is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not upload, store, or process your images on our servers
  • We do not collect or store the Data URLs you generate
  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not collect personal information to use the converter tool

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies:

  • Log Data: Your browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access times
  • Device Information: Device type, screen resolution, and general location (country/region level)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website, features used, and time spent on pages

Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements and may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history
  • Analytics Services: Help us understand website usage and improve our services

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve user experience and website functionality
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about your use of our website. For detailed information about the cookies we use and your choices regarding cookies, please see our Cookie Policy.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Since our converter tool processes images entirely in your browser, your files never leave your device, providing an additional layer of privacy and security.

Your Rights and Choices

For All Users

  • Cookie Preferences: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent tool
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ads Settings
  • Do Not Track: We honor Do Not Track signals where technically feasible

For European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

For California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to equal service and price

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International Data Transfers

Our website may be accessed from countries around the world. If you access our website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.
